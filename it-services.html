Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our IT management services

What's included in your managed IT services?

Our comprehensive managed IT services include 24/7 monitoring, helpdesk support, cybersecurity protection, cloud management, data backup, network management, and strategic IT consulting. We tailor our services to meet your specific business needs.

How do you ensure data security?

We implement multi-layered security measures including firewalls, encryption, endpoint protection, regular vulnerability assessments, employee training, and compliance with industry standards like ISO 27001 and NIST frameworks.

What industries do you specialize in?

We serve clients across healthcare, finance, manufacturing, education, professional services, and more. Our solutions are adaptable to meet industry-specific compliance requirements and operational needs.

How quickly can you respond to IT issues?

Our SLA guarantees response within 15 minutes for critical issues, 1 hour for high priority, and 4 hours for standard requests. We offer 24/7/365 support with an average resolution time of under 2 hours for most issues.

Can you help with cloud migration?

Absolutely. We provide end-to-end cloud migration services including assessment, planning, execution, and optimization across AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud platforms. Our team ensures minimal downtime and maximum ROI.

What makes your IT services different?

We combine enterprise-grade expertise with personalized service, leveraging AI and automation to deliver proactive, rather than reactive, IT management. Our strategic approach aligns technology with business objectives for measurable results.
