Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our social media marketing services.

How long does it take to see results?

Most clients see initial improvements in engagement within 30 days, with significant growth in followers and website traffic within 3-6 months. Paid advertising can produce faster results, while organic growth takes more time to build momentum.

Which platforms should my business be on?

This depends entirely on your target audience and business goals. After our initial audit, we'll recommend the 2-3 platforms that will give you the best ROI. Generally, B2C businesses thrive on Instagram and TikTok, while B2B companies see better results on LinkedIn and Twitter.

How much does social media marketing cost?

Our packages start at $1,500/month for basic management. Comprehensive strategies with content creation and advertising typically range from $3,000-$7,000/month. We customize every proposal based on your specific needs and goals.

Do you provide content creation?

Yes! Our in-house creative team handles photography, graphic design, video production, and copywriting. We can also work with your existing content or train your team to create on-brand materials.

How do you measure success?

We track KPIs like engagement rate, follower growth, website traffic, lead generation, and conversion rates. Every month you'll receive a detailed report showing progress toward your specific business goals.

Can I see examples of your work?

Absolutely! During our consultation, we'll share case studies and examples from clients in similar industries. We protect client confidentiality but can show anonymized performance data.
