Cloud Solutions FAQs

Answers to common questions about our cloud services

How do I know if my business is ready for cloud migration?

Most businesses can benefit from cloud solutions. Key indicators include: needing better remote access, facing hardware limitations, experiencing growth, or wanting to reduce IT maintenance costs. We offer free assessments to evaluate your readiness.

What's the difference between public, private, and hybrid cloud?

Public cloud uses shared infrastructure (AWS, Azure, GCP). Private cloud is dedicated to your organization. Hybrid cloud combines both. We'll recommend the best approach based on your security needs, compliance requirements, and budget.

How do you ensure data security in the cloud?

We implement multiple security layers: encryption (at rest and in transit), multi-factor authentication, network security controls, regular audits, and least-privilege access. All solutions comply with major regulations like GDPR, HIPAA, and PCI DSS where applicable.

Can you help move our existing applications to the cloud?

Yes! We specialize in "lift-and-shift" migrations (moving as-is) and cloud-native transformations (refactoring for cloud). Our team assesses each application to determine the best migration strategy with minimal disruption.

What ongoing management do you provide after migration?

Our managed services include 24/7 monitoring, performance optimization, cost management, security updates, backup management, and regular reporting. We act as an extension of your IT team.

How does cloud pricing work?

Cloud services typically use pay-as-you-go pricing based on resources consumed (compute, storage, networking). We help right-size your environment and implement cost controls to avoid surprises. Many providers also offer reserved instances for predictable workloads.
