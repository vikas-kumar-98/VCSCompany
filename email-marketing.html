Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our email marketing services

How much does email marketing cost?

Our email marketing services start at $1,500/month for basic campaigns. Pricing depends on list size, frequency, and complexity of automation. We offer custom packages tailored to your business needs and budget.

How long does it take to see results?

Most clients see measurable improvements within 30-60 days. Immediate metrics like open rates often improve quickly, while revenue impact typically becomes significant after 3-6 months of consistent execution.

Do I need an existing email list?

No, we can help you build an email list from scratch using lead magnets, website opt-ins, and other proven list-building strategies. However, if you have an existing list, we'll help you clean and segment it for better performance.

What email marketing platform do you recommend?

The best platform depends on your business type and goals. For ecommerce, we recommend Klaviyo. For B2B or service businesses, ActiveCampaign is excellent. Mailchimp works well for small businesses starting with email marketing.

How do you measure success?

We track key metrics like open rates, click-through rates, conversion rates, and revenue generated. Ultimately, we measure success by the ROI our email marketing delivers for your business.
