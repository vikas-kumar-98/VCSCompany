Frequently Asked Questions

How does your solution differ from traditional security services?

Our solution combines AI-driven threat detection with human expertise, offering real-time adaptive protection that evolves with the threat landscape. Unlike traditional signature-based systems, we use behavioral analysis to detect anomalies and prevent zero-day attacks.

What compliance standards do you support?

We fully support GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, PCI DSS 4.0, SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, and NIST frameworks. Our platform includes automated compliance reporting and evidence collection to simplify audits.

How quickly can you detect and respond to breaches?

Our average detection time is under 15 minutes for critical threats, with automated containment initiated within 5 minutes of detection. For severe incidents, our response team is engaged immediately with a 1-hour maximum response time SLA.

Do you provide security training for our developers?

Yes, we offer customized secure coding training programs with hands-on labs, CTF exercises, and real-world attack simulations. Our training covers OWASP Top 10, API security, cloud security, and threat modeling.
