Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our backup and recovery services

How often should we back up our data?

The ideal backup frequency depends on your business needs. For most organizations, we recommend:

  • Critical databases: Every 15 minutes to 1 hour
  • Important files: Every 4-6 hours
  • Standard systems: Daily
  • Full system images: Weekly

Our solutions automatically adjust based on data criticality and change frequency.

What's your recovery time objective (RTO)?

Our standard RTOs are:

  • Tier 1 (Mission-critical): 15 minutes
  • Tier 2 (Business-critical): 1 hour
  • Tier 3 (Important): 4 hours
  • Tier 4 (Standard): 24 hours

We can customize RTOs based on your specific requirements with our premium plans.

How do you protect against ransomware?

Our multi-layered ransomware protection includes:

  • Immutable backups that can't be altered or deleted
  • Air-gapped storage completely isolated from networks
  • AI-based anomaly detection to identify attack patterns
  • Multi-factor authentication for all backup access
  • Regular integrity checks for early detection
Do you support hybrid cloud environments?

Yes, we specialize in hybrid cloud backup solutions that seamlessly protect:

  • On-premises servers and workstations
  • Private cloud infrastructure
  • Public cloud workloads (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • SaaS applications (Office 365, Google Workspace)
  • Virtual environments (VMware, Hyper-V)

All managed through a single unified console.

What compliance standards do you meet?

Our solutions are designed to meet stringent compliance requirements including:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act)
  • PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard)
  • SOC 2 Type II (Service Organization Control)
  • ISO 27001 (Information Security Management)
  • FISMA (Federal Information Security Management Act)
How long do you retain backup data?

Our standard retention policies are:

  • Daily backups: 30 days
  • Weekly backups: 12 weeks
  • Monthly backups: 36 months
  • Yearly backups: 7 years

Custom retention policies are available to meet your specific regulatory or business needs, including indefinite retention for critical data.
