SEO Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our SEO services and process.

How long does it take to see results from SEO?

SEO is a long-term strategy, and results typically begin to appear within 3-6 months. However, this timeline can vary based on factors like your industry competitiveness, website age, and the current state of your SEO. We usually see initial improvements in technical SEO metrics within weeks, while ranking improvements and traffic growth take longer to materialize.

What's the difference between SEO and PPC?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) focuses on earning organic traffic through improving your website's visibility in search results. PPC (Pay-Per-Click) involves paying for ads that appear at the top of search results. While PPC delivers immediate but temporary traffic, SEO builds long-term, sustainable visibility. The most effective digital marketing strategies combine both approaches.

How do you measure SEO success?

We track multiple key performance indicators (KPIs) including: organic traffic growth, keyword rankings, click-through rates, conversion rates, bounce rates, pages per session, domain authority, backlink profile growth, and ultimately, revenue generated from organic search. We provide monthly reports with these metrics and insights for continuous improvement.

Is SEO a one-time service?

No, SEO requires ongoing effort. Search engines frequently update their algorithms, competitors are constantly optimizing their sites, and your business goals may evolve. We recommend at least 6-12 months of continuous SEO work to establish strong rankings, followed by maintenance to preserve and build upon those results.

Can I do SEO myself?

While basic SEO can be done in-house, professional SEO requires specialized knowledge, tools, and experience to execute effectively at scale. Our team stays current with algorithm updates, has access to premium tools, and brings proven strategies from working with hundreds of clients. This expertise typically delivers far better results than DIY approaches.

How does SEO integrate with other digital marketing?

SEO works synergistically with other channels: Content marketing fuels SEO with optimized material, social media can amplify content and build brand signals, PPC data informs SEO keyword strategy, email marketing can boost engagement metrics that influence rankings, and conversion rate optimization ensures SEO traffic converts effectively. We coordinate all these elements for maximum impact.
